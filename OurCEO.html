Elsa Frankel

Founder and Chief Executive Officer

Elsa recieved her autism diagnosis in January of 2019, during her 8th grade year. Suprised by her diagnosis, she struggled with accepting herself for multiple months following. However, come the May following the news, she came out as autistic through a speech to her middle school. The speech was her "first taste" of what would soon become an intense special interest surrounding neurodiversity advocacy.

Elsa continued her work through numerous other opportunities at her highschool: she co-led a workshop to teach people about what the life of an autistic person was like, led a similar informational table specifying in helpful vs harmful neurodiversity language, and shared resources through her role as "Kris" a 15 year old autistic girl in her school's production of the Curious Incident of the Dog in the Nighttime.

What is Autism?

An evolving definition defined by the collective autistic community

Autism is a developmental disability specified under the DSM-5. Autism effects how we experience the world and day to day life. Autism is not a seprate piece of us to be feared or cured, it is the very thing that shapes our unique personalities and experience; it is an important and normal part of our being.

Autism exists upon a non-linear spectrum. An autistic person is not "more or less severely autistic," but rather each autistuc individual has a unique mix of common traits that they experience. A common phrase you will hear is, "if you've met an autistic person, you've met one autistic person," meaning that each one of us has a different story from the next.
